Rooms and Suites

Explore our rooms and our affordable packages your friends and family will enjoy.

Superior Room

Guests enjoy verdant gardens or bay views. Superior Rooms are sensibly designed with indigenous materials, from sawali ceilings down to our accent lighting made from ratan.

Deluxe Room

Deluxe rooms offer a more private relaxation with your own personal veranda. They are modernly designed with indigenous materials, from Vigan tile floors to our accent lighting made from rattan.

Poolside Room

Our Poolside is recommended for family and small groups who loves to be near the sun. It can accommodate 8 to 10 persons. It has all the basic amenities, from T.V., toilet, hot and cold shower, minibar, and Wi-fi. It also has a lounge area where you can enjoy your coffee or snacks with friends and family.

Suite

Our Suite is a private space at the beach recommended for friends who loves the bathing in the sun. It can accommodate 6 to 12 persons. It has all the basic amenities, from T.V., toilet, hot and cold shower, minibar, and Wi-fi. It has a shared living room where you can enjoy your coffee or snacks with friends.
